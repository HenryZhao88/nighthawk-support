FAQ

Common questions

Quick answers to the things people ask us most. Still stuck? Use the form below.

Why won't my feed load? +

Pull down on the feed to refresh. If it still won't load, check your connection, sign out and back in from Settings → Account, or reinstall the app. Submit a request below if the issue persists.

How is the bias score calculated? +

Each story is scored using a blend of source-level lean ratings and article-level language analysis. Scores are directional, not absolute — they're meant to help you read across viewpoints, not replace your judgment.

Can I personalize my topics? +

Yes. Open You → Interests to pick topics, mute sources, and tune how much your feed adapts to your swipes.

I think a bias score is wrong. +

Tap ••• on the article and choose Report bias score, or use the form below with the article URL. We review reports weekly.

How do I delete my account? +

Go to Settings → Account → Delete account. Deletion is permanent and removes your reading history within 30 days.

Which iOS versions are supported? +

NewsHawk supports iOS 16 and later on iPhone. iPad support is in beta.
