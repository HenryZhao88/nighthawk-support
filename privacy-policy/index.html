Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information NighthawkNews collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. We try to keep it short and plain — if anything is unclear, email us at privacy@nighthawknews.app.

Effective date: January 1, 2026 · Last updated: —

1. Who we are

NighthawkNews ("NighthawkNews," "we," "us," or "our") provides a swipeable, personalized, bias-aware news app for iOS and the support website at henryzhao88.github.io/nighthawk-support. This policy covers both the app and the website.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Information you provide

  • Account information. If you create an account, we store your email address and an authentication identifier. We never see or store your password in plain text.
  • Support requests. When you submit the contact form on this site, we collect your name, email, issue category, message, and (optionally) your app version and device model. These are stored in Google Firebase Firestore so our team can respond.
  • In-app preferences. Topics, muted sources, and bias-tuning settings you configure in You → Interests.

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • Reading activity. Articles you open, swipe, save, or share — used to personalize your feed and improve recommendations.
  • Device and diagnostic data. App version, iOS version, device model, language, time zone, and crash logs. We use this to fix bugs and prioritize improvements.
  • Usage analytics. Aggregated, non-identifying metrics such as session length and feature engagement.

2.3 Information we do not collect

  • We do not collect precise location.
  • We do not collect contacts, photos, microphone, or camera data.
  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use third-party advertising trackers.

3. How we use information

  • To deliver and personalize your news feed.
  • To compute and refine bias scores.
  • To respond to support requests and account issues.
  • To detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, and security incidents.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Legal bases (EEA / UK users)

If you are in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, we process your personal data under the following legal bases: performance of a contract (delivering the app), legitimate interests (improving and securing the service), consent (where you have opted in), and compliance with legal obligations.

5. How we share information

We share personal data only with:

  • Service providers who process data on our behalf — currently Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Crashlytics) and Apple (App Store and TestFlight). These providers are bound by data-processing agreements.
  • Authorities when required by law, subpoena, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of NighthawkNews, our users, or the public.
  • A successor entity in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to the same protections in this policy.

6. Data retention

  • Account data is retained while your account is active and deleted within 30 days after you delete your account.
  • Reading history is kept for up to 18 months to power personalization, then automatically purged.
  • Support requests are retained for up to 24 months for quality assurance and may be deleted earlier on request.
  • Crash and diagnostic logs are retained for up to 90 days.

7. Security

Data is transmitted over TLS and stored on Google Cloud infrastructure with encryption at rest. Access to support data is restricted to authenticated administrators using Firebase Authentication and enforced by Firestore security rules. No system is perfectly secure — if you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately.

8. Your rights and choices

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Delete your data (in-app via Settings → Account → Delete account).
  • Export a copy of your data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@nighthawknews.app. We respond within 30 days.

9. Children's privacy

NighthawkNews is rated 12+ and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

10. International transfers

Our service providers may process your data in the United States and other jurisdictions. Where required, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent safeguards to protect your information.

11. Cookies and similar technologies

This support website uses only the local storage required for Firebase Authentication sessions. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party analytics on the website.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced in-app or by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

13. Contact us

Questions, requests, or complaints about this policy can be sent to privacy@nighthawknews.app.
